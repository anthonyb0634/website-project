Learn My Journey

How It All Started

It all started in my elementary school. I tried out for the team in the fourth grade and did not make the cut. That really got to me. So I played basketball hoping to make the team on 5th grade. I probably would of made the team but my grades were bad so I couldn't try out, that was when I learned my lesson about grades and school work.

A Dream Starts

When I got my acceptance letter it had told me that I had been accepted to Highschool of Telecommunications of Art and Technology, also known as Telecom. The home of the yellow jackets. My freshman year I had made the cut to the Junior Varsity Basketball team. I was so nervous since it was my first time being in a official organized basketball team. Everytime that my coach had tried to put me in the game I would turn it over. I averaged 0.9 points, 0.4 assists the whole season and we went 1-9 in the regular season. I was lacking in confidence to continue playing after that, so I put in hours everyday throughout the summer.

1 minor step 1 major change

My Sophomore year during the time I had in JV my second year I averaged 8 points per game and I lead the league in assists. I got moved up to the Varsity Basketball team mid-way through my JV season. The difference between the Varsity level players and Junior Varsity was not expected but luckily I tend to step up to better competition and I was known as the best on ball defender on the team. I usually guarded the best player on the other team unless if it was a center. It wasn’t easy and sometimes I had bad games. Now I wasn’t a main scorer on the tean usually the 4th option but if I did what I had to do we usually won. Even though we didnt go far in the playoffs I learned alot from my coach.

Passing The Torch

There is nothing I did more playing basketball then learning and now I want to do what people did to me and that was teach the young ones about what the future can be and how one dream can change your life. Theres nothing like having a dream and chasing it. Now I know my journey is not done. But if I can help someone see things the way I do I beileve they can go far.
